Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

EngageWorx ("we," "us," or "our") operates the EngageWorx platform (engwx.com and portal.engwx.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data. It applies to all users regardless of location, including the United States, United Kingdom, and European Economic Area (EEA).

Data Controller

For the purposes of UK GDPR and EU GDPR, the data controller is EngageWorx, based in Miami, Florida, USA. You can contact our data protection team at privacy@engwx.com.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data: account information (name, email address, company name, phone number), billing and payment data (processed securely by a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor), platform usage data (features accessed, session duration, device and browser information), communication data (messages sent through the platform on behalf of your business), technical data (IP address, cookies, log files, and analytics identifiers), and marketing preferences (opt-in status for newsletters and product updates).

Legal Bases for Processing (UK and EU)

Under UK GDPR and EU GDPR, we process personal data on the following legal bases: contractual necessity, legitimate interests, consent (for marketing communications), and legal obligation (to comply with tax, accounting, and telecommunications regulations).

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to: provide, operate, and maintain the EngageWorx platform, process subscriptions and billing, send transactional notifications, improve platform performance and develop new features, comply with applicable laws including TCPA (US), PECR (UK), ePrivacy Directive (EU), CAN-SPAM (US), and GDPR (UK/EU), respond to support requests, and detect and prevent fraud.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share data only with vetted service providers operating under data processing agreements. These include providers of carrier-grade messaging and voice infrastructure, PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processing, proprietary cloud database and authentication services, and anonymized analytics. A current list of sub-processors is available on request to privacy@engwx.com. We do not sell your personal data to third parties, and we do not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing or promotional purposes.

SMS Communications

If you provide your mobile phone number and opt in to receive SMS messages from EngageWorx, the following terms apply:

No sharing for marketing. No mobile information, including phone numbers and SMS opt-in data, will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third parties for marketing.

Message types. Messages may include account notifications, billing and invoice alerts, security alerts, support ticket updates, scheduled maintenance notices, and platform usage alerts.

Message frequency. Message frequency varies based on your account activity and notification preferences.

Costs. Message and data rates may apply, charged by your mobile carrier.

Opt-out. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for help, or contact us at hello@engwx.com or +1 (786) 982-7800.

Sub-processors. SMS messages are transmitted via vetted carrier-grade messaging infrastructure providers operating under signed data processing agreements that prohibit secondary use of your data.

Records. We retain SMS consent records — including phone number, timestamp, opt-in method, and the disclosure language shown at the time of consent — for audit and compliance purposes.

For full SMS Program Terms, see https://www.engwx.com/sms-terms.

International Data Transfers

If you are located in the UK or EEA, your data may be transferred to the United States. We safeguard these transfers using Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), UK International Data Transfer Agreements (IDTAs), and data processing agreements with all sub-processors.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, we retain data for up to 12 months for legal compliance, billing records for up to 7 years as required by tax laws, and anonymized analytics data indefinitely for service improvement.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the right to: access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion, object to or restrict certain processing, receive a copy of your data in a portable format, and withdraw consent at any time. California residents have additional rights under CCPA/CPRA. UK and EU residents may lodge a complaint with their supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@engwx.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

We use essential cookies required for platform functionality, analytics cookies (consent required in UK/EU), and preference cookies. We respect Do Not Track browser signals.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS 1.3 encryption, AES-256 encryption at rest, row-level security, regular security audits, role-based access controls, and incident response procedures with breach notification within 72 hours as required by GDPR.

Children's Privacy

EngageWorx is not directed at individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email and a prominent notice on our website.

Contact Us

For privacy inquiries: privacy@engwx.com | +1 (786) 982-7800. If you are in the UK, you may also contact the ICO at ico.org.uk. If you are in the EU, contact your local Data Protection Authority.
